About SEVAA

A non-government philanthropic organization dedicated to community service

Our Philosophy

SEVAA is inspired by Thakur-Maa-Swamiji ideals, following the principle of "Shiv Gyane Jeev Seva" - serving humanity as a form of divine worship.

Our Commitments

  • Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion
  • Collaboration & Community Engagement
  • Building self-confidence within the community
  • Responsibility & Accountability
  • Respect, Mutual Trust and Compassion
  • Integrity in everything we do

Our Four Thrust Areas

Focused approach to community development and empowerment

Education

Quality education to the underprivileged through innovative learning centers and forest schools.

Livelihood

Sustainable livelihood for the marginalized through agriculture, organic farming, and skill development.

Community

Health, sanitation and nutrition programs to improve quality of life in rural communities.

Environment

Mitigation of environmental challenges through tree plantation and conservation initiatives.
